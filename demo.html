VELURIA

Veluria is a premium digital infrastructure designed to power the next generation of independent creators. It combines payments, content delivery, security and audience ownership into one unified platform. Unlike traditional marketplaces, Veluria does not control creators — it empowers them.

Market Opportunity

The global creator economy is valued at hundreds of billions of dollars and continues to grow exponentially. Millions of creators are migrating away from platforms that extract high fees, restrict content and expose them to financial and platform risk. Veluria is positioned to become the premium backbone for this entire market.

Business Model

Veluria operates on a simple and transparent revenue model. The platform takes a 15% fee across all transactions while creators keep 85%. Revenue is generated through subscriptions, pay-per-view, digital products and future financial services.

Technology

Veluria is built with a multi-layered security and infrastructure stack including KYC verification, antifraud systems, tokenized streaming, dynamic watermarking, IP intelligence and global payment rails. This allows Veluria to operate at scale with regulatory compliance and enterprise-grade reliability.

Vision

Veluria is building the financial and technological backbone of the global creator economy. Our long-term vision is to become the Stripe and Netflix combined for independent creators, powering millions of digital businesses worldwide.
